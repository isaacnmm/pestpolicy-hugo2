What Do Cockroach Eggs Look Like?#

There are four main types of cockroaches, which commonly appear in living areas and domestic homes. They all lay eggs in sacs known as oothecae. The egg sacs, which are elongated and look similar to a pill, contains multiple eggs. Since all the egg sacs are basically the same shape, identifying them can help you determine what type of cockroaches you are dealing with in your residence. The differences are in the size and color depending on the cockroach species. The differences between the four types of roach egg sacs are described below.

  • Brown-banded cockroach - Light brown with some red. Approximately 5 mm
  • Oriental cockroach - Medium to dark reddish-brown. Approximately 8 to 10 mm.
