Understanding Chipmunk Damage#

Before diving into the best chipmunks repellents, it’s essential to understand the kind of damage chipmunks can cause.

  • These small but persistent rodents can dig up plants, chew on wires, and even find their way into homes. Their burrowing can undermine foundations and damage lawns, leading to costly repairs. By using effective repellents, you can prevent these issues and maintain a safe, chipmunk-free environment.
