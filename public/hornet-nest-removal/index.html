Found in branches of a tree, under joists, on the slide of windows, in attics, inside the usable portion of abandoned buildings or vehicles, etc. To cut the story short, any place shielded from weather and in peace can attract these hornets to build their nest. Typically, hornets prefer to build a nest in relatively high areas. These normally include, but are not at all limited to:
