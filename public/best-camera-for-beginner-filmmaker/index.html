Canon T7i uses a 3 touchscreen with a 1080p resolution to provide an HD display. 5-axis stabilization is a high-end technology incorporated in this camera to stabilize your videos whenever the camera shakes during shooting. Image stability can also be enhanced by using IS-enabled lenses. Its compatible with a variety of lenses including macro lenses, STM lenses, telephoto lenses, macro lenses, and wide-angle lenses.
