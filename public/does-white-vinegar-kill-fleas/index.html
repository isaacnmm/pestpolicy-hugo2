Alternative remedies That will kill fleas#

For the safety of your pets, you might need to consider some non-chemical-free repellents - coconut oil is one of them.

  • By definition, coconut oil is non-toxic and a natural solution that will kill fleas.
  • Luckily, applying the coconut oil is quite easy - simply rub that oil deep into the pets skin, coat, under the fur. The oil contains lauric acid that has repellent capabilities and hence most of the pests will jump off your pet. So, I advise that you apply that coconut oil on your pet while it’s in the outdoor spaces so that the fleas won’t jump back into your house.
